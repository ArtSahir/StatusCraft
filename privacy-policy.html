Privacy Policy

StatusCraft is built to be respectful with your data. This page explains what we collect, what we don't, and the third-party services we rely on.

Effective May 6, 2026 Developer · CONPLY

01 Introduction

StatusCraft (“the app”) is published by CONPLY, operated by EL MAGHNOUJI HABIL (Morocco). This policy describes the personal information we handle when you use the app on Android, the choices you have, and the third-party services that power features such as cloud sync, advertising, and crash reporting. By using StatusCraft you agree to the practices described here.

Last updated: May 6, 2026.

02 What we collect

StatusCraft works without an account, so we do not ask for your name, phone number, address book, or location. The data the app does see falls into three buckets:

On-device data (never leaves your phone)

  • Favorites, diary notes, daily schedules, and theme/notification preferences are stored locally using Android DataStore and Room database.
  • The list of dismissed in-app notifications and recent search categories.

Aggregate, non-identifying data sent to our backend

  • Anonymous share/copy counts per phrase so we can show a global “Most shared” section. We use Firebase Firestore for this. No user id is attached.

Diagnostic & analytics data (only after you opt in)

  • Crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics and aggregate event counters via Google Analytics for Firebase. Both stay disabled until you flip the “Usage analytics” toggle in Settings.

03 How we use it

We use the data above strictly to operate, secure, and improve StatusCraft — never to build advertising profiles or sell information. Specifically:

  • Display the content you requested.
  • Remember your language, theme, and feature preferences.
  • Surface community trends like the “Most shared” rail.
  • Diagnose crashes (only with your consent).
  • Show ads to keep the app free.

04 Third-party services

StatusCraft integrates a small number of vetted services. Each one has its own policy:

Google Play ServicesRequired Android system services on the device.
FirebaseFirestore, Remote Config, App Check, Cloud Messaging.
Google AdMobBanner, interstitial, native and app-open ads.
Google UMP SDKEU/UK/CH consent form for personalized ads.
Firebase CrashlyticsCrash and ANR reports (opt-in).
Analytics for FirebaseAggregate usage events (opt-in).
Play Integrity APIApp-Check attestation; verifies the app is genuine.

05 Ads & consent

StatusCraft shows ads through Google AdMob. AdMob may collect a limited set of identifiers (Android Advertising ID, IP address, ad-interaction events) to show ads and measure performance. If you live in the EEA, UK, or Switzerland, the app uses the Google UMP consent form on first launch. Android 13+ “Delete advertising ID” switch in Settings → Privacy → Ads is honoured automatically.

06 Storage & retention

Local data lives only on your device and is wiped when you uninstall or clear storage. Anonymous share counts in Firestore are retained as long as the feature is operational. Crash and analytics events follow Firebase's default retention windows.

07 Children

StatusCraft is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has used the app and provided data to a third-party service such as AdMob, please email us.

08 Your rights

Depending on where you live, you may have rights under the GDPR, the CCPA, or similar local laws. To exercise these rights:

  • Toggle off “Usage analytics” in Settings to stop collection.
  • Use Android's “Delete advertising ID” control to disable personalized ads.
  • Uninstall the app to remove all local data.
  • Email us at the address below for any further request.

09 Security

Traffic between the app and Firebase travels over TLS 1.2+. We use Firebase App Check with the Play Integrity API to verify requests come from a genuine StatusCraft install.

10 Changes to this policy

When we change anything material we update the “Last updated” date and publish the new version at the same URL. Continued use after a revision means you accept the revised policy.

11 Contact us

Questions, requests, or concerns? Reach out and we’ll respond within a reasonable time.
